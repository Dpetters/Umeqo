Safety and Security

We understand that your data is sensitive. That is why we pledge to never track your activity on the site, and are making all of the recruiter sessions run on a secure connection (https).

What is Umeqo?

A single resource for both discovering top students and promoting your firm. We are currently in student beta mode, and will be launching to employers in just a couple of weeks!

What can I do using Umeqo?

Search, Filter, Find

Easily discover the perfect candidate from the vast collection of students. We present you with not only a candidate’s resume, but also info applicable to your firm, such as event attendance. Potential candidates can be added to custom resume books that can be emailed and/or downloaded.

Effectively plan events

The Umeqo Event Scheduling Assistant presents you with an overview of both major campus activities and events hosted by other companies, allowing you to schedule your event at an optimal time. Umeqo also handles event check-ins, minimizing headaches and the length of those check-in lines.

Learn about the campus

With 400+ campus organizations documented, you will be able to click on those that any student is involved in to learn more about them.

Discover promising candidates

Umeqo allows you to annotate students’ profiles, making it easy for you to remember promising candidates and share notes with colleagues.
