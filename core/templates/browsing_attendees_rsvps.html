{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Browsing Attendees and RSVPs {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: From your homepage, click on the event or deadline whose RSVPs and attendees you wish to browse.

All of your upcoming and past events and deadlines are at the bottom, with past ones dimmed out.

Step 2: You can either click a "View All" link or click on the students one by one.

Browse RSVPs....

...and Attendees.

Note that some students appear grayed out. These students do not yet have Umeqo accounts and thus are not filterable/searchable. However, you can always export their info.

Step 3: Switch to other event student lists quickly using the "Choose Student List" section.

Step 4: Instead of browsing through an event student list, you can also export it.

Simply select between a text and excel-compatible CSV format and decide whether you want to email or download the file.

Step 5: Once you have jumped to the event list you want, you can start browsing the students.

{% endblock %} {% block things_to_note %}

2. Not every student has a Umeqo account, so the attendee count may be higher than the number of results. Those who are not have already received an email telling them that {% if user.recruiter %}{{user.recruiter.employer}}{% else %}you{% endif %} can only see their name and email until they register.
