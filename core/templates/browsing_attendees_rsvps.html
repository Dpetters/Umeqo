{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Browsing Attendees and RSVPs {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: From your homepage, click on the event or deadline whose RSVPs and attendees you wish to browse.

All of your upcoming and past events and deadlines are at the bottom, with past ones dimmed out.

Step 2: You can either click a "View All" link or click on the students one by one.

Step 3: Switch students at anytime by choosing the appropriate one in the "Choose Student List" section.

{% endblock %} {% block things_to_note %}

1. If you filter by an attribute which a student has not yet filled out, that student will not appear in the results. e.g. If you filter for students with an SAT of 2000 or above, any student who hasn't specified his/her SAT will not appear in the results.

2. If you have a free trial subscription, then you will only be able to search and browse your events' RSVPs and attendees.
