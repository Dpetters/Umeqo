Umeqo

Coming to MIT Fall 2011

Umeqo is an innovative recruiting resource for students and employers to discover one another.

What’s so innovative?

Easy-to-Maintain Profile. Fill in just a few fields to be discovered by employers. Umeqo indexes your resume and makes your profile searchable so employers can easily find you.

Streamlined Events. Add events to Google Calendar with a click of a button, view and print location maps - never again be misinformed of what to bring and what to wear.

Automatic Notifications. Subscribe to employers you’re interested in and get notified when they post new events and deadlines.

Event Invites. Receive invites to both public and private events from employers interested in you.

We're working hard to craft the ultimate experience.
