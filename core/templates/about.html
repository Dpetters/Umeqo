What is Umeqo?

Umeqo is a recruiting resource meant to help students and employers discover one another. By streamlining the familiar paradigms of resumes and events, we are looking to save both parties time and effort.

Why was Umeqo created?

Umeqo was created by three current MIT undergraduates (Dmitrij Petters, Zac Dearing, and Josh Ma) to fix the pains of recruiting at MIT. We were frustrated about missing recruiting events because we didnâ€™t hear about them and having to drop our resumes in a gazillion different places. What lies ahead?

With the Beta released to students, campus organizations, and employers, we are busy garnening feedback, adding new features, and tweaking things here and there. With student and recruiter support, we hope that Umeqo becomes the de-facto standard event management and student discovery tool at MIT.
