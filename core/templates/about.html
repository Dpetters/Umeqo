What is Umeqo?

Umeqo is a new faster and smarter way for recruiters to find potential student candidates. Our mission is to make campus recruiting simpler and less time consuming for all three parties involved - recruiters, career offices, and students.

Why was Umeqo created?

Umeqo was originally built by a group of students from MIT who experienced firsthand the deficiencies with the way campus recruiting was done and decided to do something about it.

The Umeqo team is now actively working alongside career offices, recruiters, and students to build the fast, smart, and easy-to-use recruiting platform that they deserve.
