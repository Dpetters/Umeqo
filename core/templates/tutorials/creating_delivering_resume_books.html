{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Creating and Delivering Resume Books {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: From your homepage, begin by searching by keyword or clicking "Browse Students".

There is also a "Browse Students" link in the side navigation menu on the left.

Step 2: Filter students by a variety of attributes grouped into four different categories.

Use this to find students who satisfy a particular requirement, such as speaking a language or being a graduating senior.

Step 3: Search students' resumes by keyword/skills, or search for individuals by first/last name.

Results will by default be ordered by relevance. However, you can also modify in what order and how many results appear.

Step 4: Add and remove students to and from your custom resume book one by one or in batches.

Students will never know that you added them or removed them from any resume book. Nothing that you do is recorded.

To toggle students in and out of the resume book one by one, click on the little green pluses and red crosses, respectively.

To add and remove multiple students from the resume book at once, check the checkboxes next to their name and click the corresponding button. Checking the checkbox in the menu bar (on the left of the "Add to Resume Book" button) selects all students on the page.

Step 5: View the contents of any resume book.

Select the "Current Resume Book" student list or click the student count to view students in the current resume book.

Select the name of any past resume book under "Choose Student List" to see its contents.

Step 6: Once you are satisfied with your selections, click "Deliver" to open the resume book delivery dialog.

Students will never know that you added them or removed them from any resume book. Nothing that you do is recorded.

Step 7: Download your resume book or email it to one or more email addresses.

For ease of reference, try to give each resume book as specific of a custom name as possible.

Select "email" as the delivery type to email the resume book to one or more email addresses.

Step 8: Re-deliver or delete any of your past resume books.

The easiest way to access past resume book is to use the "Resume Book History" link on the homepage.

All re-delivered resume books will have the latest copies of all the included students' resumes.
