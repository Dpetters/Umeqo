{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Managing Credentials {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: Access your account settings from the "Account" dropdown on the upper right.

There is also a "Account Settings" link in the side navigation menu on the left, underneath "Account".

Step 2: Open the credential creation dialog by clicking on the links at the bottom.

Step 3: Create an account for a co-worker by filling out all the fields.

The credentials will be immediately available for use.

Step 4: Delete your account by clicking on the "Delete Account" link.

Please note that if you are the only recruiter with an account at your firm, you cannot delete your account. Create an account for a co-worker before deleting yours, so that your firm does not lose access to the site.
