{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Getting an Event Subscription {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: Access your account settings from the "Account" dropdown on the upper right.

There is also a "Account Settings" link in the side navigation menu on the left, underneath "Account".

Step 2: Click on the "Subscription & Billing" tab to view your current subscription plan.

Step 3: Click the "Modify Subscription" button.

Step 4: Click the "Learn More" button in the event subscription block.

The exact wording of the button depends on whether you are currently subscribed and whether your subscription is active or not.

Step 5: Fill out the subscription request form and we will send you further instructions.

In particular, we will send you the price and terms of the subscription, terms of service, and instructions on how to make payment.

  • The exact pricing depends on multiple factors.
