{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Deleting Your Account {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: Access your account settings from the "Account" dropdown on the upper right.

There is also a "Account Settings" link in the side navigation menu on the left, underneath "Account".

Step 2: Delete your account by clicking on the "Delete Account" link.

Please note that if you are the only recruiter with an account at your firm, you cannot delete your account. If such is the case, then you have to create an account for a co-worker before deleting yours, so that your firm does not lose access to the site.

The screenshot below is not clickable if you are the only recruiter with an account at your firm and thus unable to delete your account.
