{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Sending Invitations {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: From your homepage, begin by searching by keyword or clicking "Browse Students".

There is also a "Browse Students" link in the left side navigation menu.

Step 2: Switch to other student lists quickly using the "Choose Student List" section.

Step 3: Browse through students to find the ones that you wish to invite.

To invite a single student, click on the "Invite to Event" link on the right of the student result.

To invite multiple students at once, check the checkboxes and select "Send Invitations" from the "More Actions" dropdown above the results.

If inviting multiple students, make sure to also select which upcoming event or deadline you wish to invite them to.

Step 4: Come up with a personalized message for the student(s) and send the invitation.
