{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Creating Accounts for Co-Workers {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: Access your account settings from the "Account" dropdown on the upper right.

There is also a "Account Settings" link in the side navigation menu on the left, underneath "Account".

Step 2: Open the account creation dialog by clicking on the links at the bottom.

Step 3: Create an account for a co-worker by filling out all the fields.

The account will be immediately available for use, meaning that your co-worker can use the credentials right after you submit the form.

{% endblock %} {% block things_to_note %}
