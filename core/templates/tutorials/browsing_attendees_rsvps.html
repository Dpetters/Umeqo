{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Browsing Attendees and RSVPs {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1:From your homepage, click on "Browse" in the left side menu to browse all of your events.

If you want to browse RSVPs from an upcoming event, simply pick the one you want from the home page.

Step 2:Click on the "Past" link on the left hand side to view your past events. Choose one.

Step 3: Choose which group of students you wish to look at using the tabs in the event admin.

Note that the groups (and thus tabs) are titled slighly differently for deadlines and private events.

Step 4: You can either click a "View All" link or click on the students one by one.

Note that some students appear grayed out. These students do not yet have Umeqo accounts and thus are not filterable/searchable. However, you can always export their info.

Step 5: Switch to other event student lists quickly using the "Choose Student List" section.

Step 6: Once you have jumped to the event list you want, you can start browsing the students.
