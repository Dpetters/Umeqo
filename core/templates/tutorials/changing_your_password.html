{% extends 'tutorial_base.html' %} {% block tutorial_title %} Changing Your Password {% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Step 1: Access your account settings from the "Account" dropdown on the upper right.

There is also a "Account Settings" link in the side navigation menu on the left, underneath "Account".

Step 2: Fill out the form underneath the "Change Password" header.

If for some reason you do not remember your current password, visit the password reset page.
