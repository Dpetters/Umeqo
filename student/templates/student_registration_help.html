There are a few possible reasons for why you cannot register.

  • You are not a student.

    The student side of Umeqo is for students only. This means that employers, affiliates, group lists, professors and alumni are all not eligible to register as students.

  • You are a freshman or a student taking a temporary leave from MIT.

    For these reasons, you may be marked as an affiliate in the MIT database. If this is so, please contact us.

  • Other reasons that we are not yet aware of.

    Judge for yourself as to whether recruiters should be able to see you alongside other students, and let us know of your situation.
