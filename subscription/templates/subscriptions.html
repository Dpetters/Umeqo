Umeqo Features At a Glance

Search, Filter, Find

Umeqo presents not only a student’s resume, but also info applicable to your firm, such as event attendance. Potential candidates can be added to custom resume books that can be emailed and/or downloaded.

Effectively Plan Events

Umeqo not only handles check-ins, but also warns you if a major campus activity or an event hosted by another company overlaps with yours, allowing you to pick an optimal time.

Learn About Each Campus

With hundreds of campus organizations documented, you will be able to click on those that any student is involved in to learn more about them.

Discover Promising Candidates

Umeqo allows you to star and annotate students’ profiles, making it easy for you to remember promising candidates and share notes with colleagues.
