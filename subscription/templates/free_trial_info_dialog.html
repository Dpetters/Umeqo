The Umeqo Event Subscription is reserved for employers that partner with campus organizations to host campus events.

You can follow the steps below to get access -

  • Plan a campus event (competition, info session, etc) for students.
  • Reach out to a campus organization to help you host the event.
  • Have the organization use Umeqo to manage RSVPs and check-ins.
  • After the event, you will be provided with credentials to Umeqo.

Here are a few campus organizations whose primary function is to help employers plan campus events -

  • SBC (sbc@mit.edu)
  • Traders@MIT (traders@mit.edu)
  • MIT SWE (swe-exec@mit.edu)
