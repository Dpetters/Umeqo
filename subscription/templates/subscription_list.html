Umeqo Subscription Choices

What is Umeqo?

Umeqo is a new recruiting resource that makes discovering and connecting with students easier than ever. Currently available only at MIT, it empowers recruiters to easily filter and search student profiles, create custom resume books, and manage both public and private events.

Who else is using Umeqo?

Hundreds of students (soon-to-be the entire campus!), a dozen top firms, and campus organizations are all using Umeqo to exchange information and help bring about a better recruiting experience for all.

Safety and Security

We understand that your data is sensitive.
That is why we take extra care to never collect any statistics about what you do on the site. Your activity will stay completely anonymous to students.

Umeqo Features At a Glance

Search, Filter, Find

Umeqo presents not only a student’s resume, but also info applicable to your firm, such as event attendance. Potential candidates can be added to custom resume books that can be emailed and/or downloaded.

Effectively Plan Events

Umeqo not only handles check-ins, but also presents you with an overview of both major campus activities and events hosted by other companies, allowing you to pick an optimal time for your event.

Learn About the Campus

With 435+ campus organizations documented, you will be able to click on those that any student is involved in to learn more about them.

Discover Promising Candidates

Umeqo allows you to annotate students’ profiles, making it easy for you to remember promising candidates and share notes with colleagues.
