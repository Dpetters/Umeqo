Umeqo Subscription Choices

What is Umeqo?

Umeqo is an innovative recruiting resource for students and employers to discover one another. The platform empowers employers to easily filter and search student profiles, create resume books, and post and manage events and deadlines.

Who else is using Umeqo?

Hundreds of students (soon-to-be the entire campus!), a dozen top firms, and MIT campus organizations are all using Umeqo to exchange information and help bring about a better recruiting experience for all.

Safety and Security

We understand that your data is sensitive.
That is why we pledge to never track your activity on the site. We will never collect statistics about anything you do, and all of your data will stay 100% confidential.

Umeqo Features At a Glance

Search, Filter, Find

Umeqo presents not only a student’s resume, but also info applicable to your firm, such as event attendance. Potential candidates can be added to custom resume books that can be emailed and/or downloaded.

Effectively Plan Events

Umeqo not only handles check-ins, but also presents you with an overview of both major campus activities and events hosted by other companies, allowing you to pick an optimal time for your event.

Learn About the Campus

With 420+ campus organizations documented, you will be able to click on those that any student is involved in to learn more about them.

Discover Promising Candidates

Umeqo allows you to annotate students’ profiles, making it easy for you to remember promising candidates and share notes with colleagues.
